Delete Your FinTrack Account

Option 1: Delete from the App

Steps:

  1. Open the FinTrack app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Scroll down and tap Delete Account
  4. Confirm deletion

Note: All your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

Option 2: Request Deletion via Email

If you cannot access the app, email us at:

Email: rmialmbs@gmail.com

Subject: Delete My FinTrack Account

Include: The email address associated with your account

We will process your request within 7 business days.

What Gets Deleted

  • Your account and authentication credentials
  • All transaction data
  • Budget and savings goals
  • Account settings and preferences
  • Cloud-synced data (if enabled)
